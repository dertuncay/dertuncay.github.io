Welcome!

I am a Researcher at the National Institute of Oceanography and Applied Geophysics - OGS in the Center for Seismological Research. My current research interests are in the dynamic characterisation of structures and simplified structural modelling for rapid damage estimation.
I am also interested in strong motion seismology, especially in near-fault regions.
Furthermore, I am interested in machine learning applications in seismology.
Finally, I am working on data analysis in DAS datasets.
