Welcome!

I am Post-Doc at National Institute of Oceanography and Applied Geophysics - OGS in Center for Seismological Research. My research interests are mostly on the behavior of the seismic signals that are recorded at very close to the ruptured fault. I am looking for impulsive signals that are recorded at strong earthquakes.
Furthermore, I am interested in machine learning applications on seismology.
