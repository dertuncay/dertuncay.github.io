Welcome!

I am Post-Doc at the National Institute of Oceanography and Applied Geophysics - OGS in Center for Seismological Research. My current research interests are on dynamic characterisation of structures and simplified structural modelling for rapid damage estimation purposes.
I am also interested in strong motion seismology, especially in near-fault regions.
Furthermore, I am interested in machine learning applications in seismology.
Finally, I am working on data analysis in DAS datasets.
